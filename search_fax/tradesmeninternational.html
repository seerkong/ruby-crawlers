Construction Staffing in Florida

Are you looking for proven craftsmen to support your core skilled employees for a short-term project? Do you want to avoid increasing your workers’ comp exposure and eliminate costs and hassles associated with adding permanent payroll? Want to maximize workforce productivity… your bottom line?

If you are looking for construction staffing in Florida, Tradesmen International has the solution to all of your skilled labor staffing needs. With five convenient offices in Ft. Lauderdale, Jacksonville, Ocala, Orlando and Tampa, we can fill any need for craftsmen to add to your skilled construction staff anywhere in Florida.

We take great pride in recruiting only the most skilled, safe and reliable craftsmen. You can rest assured that our skilled workers have undergone an exhaustive hiring process including a thorough interview, background check and challenging hands-on professional aptitude tests. All of our employees are subject to routine monthly evaluations to guarantee their skills remain up to our demanding standards.

Marine Construction Staffing in Florida

Tradesmen International also recognizes that the marine construction industry requires craftsmen with a special skill set. We have an office dedicated to marine construction staffing in Florida. We have a marine construction workforce with deep experience in the marine industry and supply all marine trades such as shipfitters, stick/tig/mig welders, pipe welders, aluminum hull welders, marine electricians and many more. All of our marine craftsmen are held to the same exacting standards as our regular construction force. If you need skilled construction professionals at your shipyard to complement your core craftsmen, we offer the best marine construction staffing in Florida.

Do you need additional skilled craftsmen at your site or shipyard? Contact one of our Florida locations today.
